Core Values

  • Excellence: Strive for evidence-based practice, competency, continuous improvement
  • Adaptability and flexibility in all functions and activities
  • Integrity: Strive for honesty, truthfulness, credibility, and sincerity
  • Professionalism: Demonstrate expertise, proficiency, and promote teamwork
  • Accountability and Transparency: Endeavour for openness, liability, in fiscal and social responsibility

Current Priority

Our current priority among many is the development of the public health workforce through a fellowship program.
